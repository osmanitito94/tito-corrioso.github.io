Osmani Tito-Corrioso received the B.Sc. in Mathematic degree from University of Oriente, Cuba in 2017, and the M.Sc. in Mathematical Science degree from University of Havana in 2022. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree at University of Oriente.


He is Professor in the University of Matanzas, Cuba. His research interest area includes Post-quantum Cryptography, Cryptanalysis, Mathematical Modeling, etc.
